Our Places of Worship

1889 HSDA ATAPAR-OLAM CHURCH

Gotojyang Parish, Olilim Sub-County, Otuke District

This humble congregation, is so far the only 1889 HSDA church in Lango Sub-region, it is growing and understands its mission of evangelizing Lango Sub-Region. A simple church structure is under construction, and will soon be holding evangelistic campaigns within Otuke and the rest of Lango Be Blessed to Join them for fellowship if you Happen to be around there

1889 HSDA AKISIM CHURCH

Katakwi District, Toroma Sub-county, Akisim Village

A humble Home church with a membership of about 9 adults and 2 children, it is a growing church with almost a half an acre of land available for church construction, Image taken during school time, Most members were at school. Please feel free to Join them for worship

1889 HSDA ANAKA CHURCH

Northern Uganda, Nwoya District

1889 HSDA Church Anaka is located about 200 meters after Nwoya District Administration offices along Anaka-Amuru Road. Feel free to Join us for fellowship if you happen to visit northern Uganda

1889 HSDA Kalagi

Mukono Uganda

A humble home Church around Central Uganda

1889 HSDA Ariamareng

Katakwii, Usuk, Uganda

Ariamareng 1889 HSDA Uganda is a church with promising youth, though strugling, it is one of the churches where if all talents are rightly directed will be able to accomplish much, always pray for this church and whenever possible make time to go fellowship with them

Nkoone 1889 HSDA Church

Buyende, Irundu, Uganda

Here you will meet men of interesting stories of their journey of Faith, you will be enjoying God's wonderous works of creation as you view Lake Kyoga at just a storn's throw. They are welcoming. Be sure to visit then when you are around Busoga Sub-Region
